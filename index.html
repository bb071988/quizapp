Guitar Quiz App

This quiz allows you to test your knowledge of famous guitars used by famous musicians. The app will give you a little background on the musician and show you their picture. Your job is to guess the name of their most famous guitar. The quiz will give you four possible names for each musician's guitar for you to choose from.
Click on the guitar name you think is right to make your guess.
Good Luck!
